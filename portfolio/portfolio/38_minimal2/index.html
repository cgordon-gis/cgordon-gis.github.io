Minimal

Minimal map of the River Kemp catchment, South Shropshire

November 2025

Data

  • River Kemp watershed polygon from Map with a new tool in 2022.
  • OpenStreetMap data for places, waterways and natural:water downloaded via QuickOSM plugin.
  • Environment Agency Lidar data - 2m DSM composite
    • Attribution statement: Â© Environment Agency copyright and/or database right 2022. All rights reserved. Open Government Licence.

Tools used

  • QGIS.

What did I learn?

  • It’s surprisingly difficult to be minimal! Initially added too many extras, but it became clear they weren’t needed once I created the layout.

What could I do differently or want to find out?

  • Waterways look rather messy, I’d like to check those. Can I simplify them?

Process (more detail about how I made the map)

  • Merged lidar data tiles then clipped to mask with the watershed polygon.
  • Styled clipped lidar as hillshade.
  • Select by location to intersect the places, waterways and water with the watershed polygon.
  • Styled waterways and water as blue and labelled waterways.
  • Styled places with no style and labelled.
  • Set up quick layout and exported.
