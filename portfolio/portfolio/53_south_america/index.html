South America

Observations of glass frogs of Central and South America: Hyalinobatrachium

Data

  • Downloaded from AmphibiaWeb.
  • Photos from Wikimedia Commons

Tools

  • QGIS.

Process

  • Data was provided as tab-separated text, which needed a bit of cleaning up before importing in QGIS.
  • Once I’d decided what data to use, it was mainly a question of creating the layout. Green and yellow for frogs!

What would I like to do differently?

I didn’t want to spend too much time looking for data, so ended up just going with one genus of frog - Hyalinobatrachium. Would also be interesting to do something with areas of distribution rather than points. Or maybe more species/genus, but maybe distribution per country.
