GIS notes

Notes are created for the benefit of my students in the School of Earth and Environment at the University of Leeds.

  • Frequently asked questions (FAQ) - answers to questions that I'm asked frequently by students, but which aren't in the regular workbooks yet.
  • Useful mapping links - links to freely available maps and data on the web.
  • Video clips - links to video clips to support the GIS and graphics workbooks.
  • Downloads - materials that I have created for use by students and staff at Leeds. Note that some have restricted access.
